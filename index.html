How It Works

📺

Step 1 · Watch

Each module links to the best YouTube tutorial for that topic — handpicked for clarity. We tell you exactly which part to watch.

🔧

Step 2 · Modify It

After watching, you get 3 challenges that push you to change, break, and rebuild. This is where the real learning happens — not the watching.

🧠

Why This Works

Passive watching feels like learning but isn't. The moment you make something break and then fix it, you own that concept forever.

⭐

Bonus Challenges

Every module ends with a bonus — harder and more open-ended. Skip it if you're still on the main 3. Come back when things click.

Challenge Difficulty Guide

1
Guided Change A specific, achievable modification. Follow the pattern from the tutorial — builds confidence and reinforces what you just saw.
2
Requires Thinking You'll need to experiment and problem-solve. The tutorial won't give you the exact answer — you have to figure it out.
3
Open-Ended No single right answer. You make the design decisions. This is where you go from copying to creating.
⭐
Bonus — Go Deeper More complex and more rewarding. For when the first three feel comfortable and you're hungry for more.
